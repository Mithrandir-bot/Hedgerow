Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about promotional risk coverage.

Is this gambling?
No. Hedgerow is a licensed risk coverage product. You're transferring promotional risk to us, just like buying any other business insurance. We're licensed and regulated in the State of Florida. You never place a bet or open a trading account.
How do you determine pricing?
Our pricing is based on real-time probability data from regulated prediction markets, combined with your specific promotion parameters. Higher-probability events cost more to cover (just like insurance for higher-risk scenarios). Our calculator gives you an instant, transparent quote.
How fast do I get paid if the event triggers?
Within 24 hours of the event resolving. For sports outcomes, this is typically same-day. For weather events, payout processes the morning after. Funds are sent via ACH to your business bank account.
What if I want to cancel my coverage?
You can cancel any coverage up to 24 hours before the event for a partial refund (minus a 15% administrative fee). Within 24 hours of the event, coverage is non-refundable.
What events can I get coverage for?
Any event with a clear, verifiable outcome: sports game results, weather conditions (rain, temperature, snow), economic indicators (gas prices, interest rate decisions), and more. If we can verify it happened, we can cover it. Custom triggers available on Enterprise plans.
Is there a minimum or maximum coverage amount?
Minimum coverage is $250 exposure. Maximum per-event coverage is currently $25,000 (higher limits available on Enterprise plans). No limit on the number of events you can cover per month.
