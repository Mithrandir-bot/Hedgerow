Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about event-based risk coverage.

Is this gambling or insurance?
Neither, exactly. Hedgerow is a risk-transfer product. You are paying a premium to offset a specific, verifiable business risk — similar in principle to insurance, but structured differently. You never place a bet or open a trading account. Hedgerow is a licensed entity operating under Florida state regulations.
How is the premium calculated?
Premiums are based on real-time probability data from regulated markets combined with your specific exposure amount. Higher-probability events cost more to cover (the same way higher-risk insurance costs more). Our calculator gives you a transparent, instant estimate.
How fast do I get paid?
Choose your payout method: USDC stablecoin settles same-hour, and USD via ACH arrives same business day. No paper checks, no multi-day waits. You pick your preferred method when you set up coverage.
What events can I cover?
Any event with a clear, verifiable outcome: sports results, weather conditions (rain, temperature, snow), economic indicators (gas prices, interest rate decisions), commodity prices, and more. If we can independently verify the outcome, we can cover it.
Can I cancel a policy?
You can cancel coverage more than 24 hours before the event for a partial refund (minus a 15% administrative fee). Within 24 hours of the event, coverage is non-refundable.
What are the coverage limits?
Minimum exposure is $250. Standard per-event coverage caps at $25,000. Higher limits are available on Enterprise plans. There is no limit on the number of events you can cover per month.
