Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about running a Hedgerow promotion.

Is this insurance?
No. Hedgerow is a marketing promotion tool. You run a promo and we fund the payoff for a flat fee. We don't insure business losses, and this isn't an insurance product, a policy, or coverage. Think of it as a marketing spend that lets you offer a bold, event-based deal to your customers.
What exactly is Hedgerow?
Hedgerow is a simple marketing tool for businesses of every kind. You run a fun promotion tied to a real public event — a team winning, rain on your event day, a hot weekend, a gas-price swing. You offer the deal to your customers; if the event happens, Hedgerow pays YOUR business the payoff within 24 hours so you can cover the giveaway at no cost to you. If it doesn't, you keep the upside from the promotion. You pay one flat fee upfront, and that's it. (We pay your business, not your customers directly.)
How is the flat fee calculated?
Your flat fee depends on how much the promo could cost you (your exposure) and the estimated odds of the event. You don't set the odds — those come from the event you pick, estimated from real-world data. More likely events cost a bit more, since the payoff is more likely to happen. The calculator on this page gives you an instant, transparent estimate, and your final flat fee uses live market data when you set up your promo. There's nothing else to pay.
How do you know if it happened?
Every promotion settles on an independent, official source — the official game result and standings for sports, the National Weather Service for weather, and the U.S. Energy Information Administration / AAA published price for gas and fuel. The moment the event is officially decided, your payout is automatic. No disputes, no judgment calls.
How fast do I get paid?
Once the event is officially decided, we pay YOU — your business — within 24 hours, by bank transfer (ACH) or USDC. No paper checks, no multi-day waits. You use that to cover the giveaway you offered your customers. You pick your preferred method when you set up your promotion.
What events can I build a promotion around?
Fun, local events with a clear, verifiable result: your team winning this weekend or by 10+, your team making the playoffs or winning the division, rain on your event day, a 90°+ weekend, a storm canceling an outdoor day, local gas topping or dropping below a set price, a record-hot weekend, or a big game-day crowd. Pick from our curated menu, or describe your own.
Can I cancel a promotion?
You can cancel more than 24 hours before the event for a partial refund of your flat fee (minus a 15% administrative fee). Within 24 hours of the event, your promotion is non-refundable.
What are the payoff limits?
The minimum payoff is $250. Standard promotions fund payoffs up to $25,000. Higher limits are available on Enterprise plans. There's no limit on how many promotions you can run per month.
